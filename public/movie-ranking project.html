The Best Movies According To Asif

My top 3 movies of all time.

Whiplash

Whiplash is a gripping drama that follows a young, ambitious jazz drummer, Andrew, as he strives to achieve greatness under the harsh and demanding mentorship of a ruthless music instructor, Fletcher.

The Dark Knight

The Dark Knight follows Batman as he battles the Joker, a criminal mastermind who seeks to plunge Gotham into chaos.

The Departed

The Departed is a tense crime thriller about an undercover cop and a mole in the police force trying to identify each other while infiltrating a Boston crime syndicate.
